In the document classes~\texttt{article} and \texttt{report} only the footer is
used. The page number is centred there. The document class~\texttt{book}, on
the other hand, uses living column titles. In addition to the page number, the
current bullet point also appears in the header. By means of the package~\ctanref{fancyhdr},
this behaviour can be can also be activated for other classes. In the file~\texttt{latex/fancyhdr.tex}
of the template is already prepared for this. You only have to remove the
comment characters before the passage in Listing~\ref{lst:final:fancyhdr}.

chapter, the simple form of page numbering is still applied.
